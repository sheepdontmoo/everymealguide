# Concrete starting picks progress - 2026-06-24

## Mission

Help visitors answer "What meal delivery service should I use?" within 60 seconds.

## Local change

- Added a homepage "Best first clicks" section.
- It gives concrete starting points for:
  - no-cook prepared meals
  - cook-yourself meal kits
  - budget meal kits
  - UK recipe boxes
- Each card has `data-decision-path` tracking through the existing click event pipeline.

## Why it matters

Visitors should not have to decode the whole market before seeing a useful brand-style example. This turns abstract categories into recognizable first clicks while still framing them as starting lanes, not fake universal winners.
