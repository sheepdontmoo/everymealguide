How brands can help

Send decision-useful information, not marketing fog.

Readers need practical buying details. The strongest partner notes are specific, current, and easy to verify.

  1. Confirm availabilityCountry, region, postcode, delivery day, and any excluded areas.
  2. Clarify meal formatCook-yourself kit, heat-and-eat meal, frozen meal, diet plan, family box, or grocery shortcut.
  3. Explain price honestlyIntro discount, normal recurring price, delivery fees, minimum order, and serving count.
  4. Share compliance-safe claimsNo unsupported medical, weight-loss, allergen, or nutrition claims.
  5. Provide approved linksPartner offer links, network name, approval date, and any required disclosure wording.

What we will not do

No fake testing

No fake discounts

We do not present an offer as current or exclusive without a source that supports it.

No commission-only ranking

Commercial relationships do not replace delivery fit, meal format, pricing, and cancellation checks.
