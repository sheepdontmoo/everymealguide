# Decision checklist progress - 2026-06-24

## Mission

Help visitors answer "What meal delivery service should I use?" within 60 seconds and avoid bad decisions caused by first-box discount hype.

## Local change

- Added a homepage "60-second rule" checklist.
- The checklist teaches the decision order:
  1. Pick the format.
  2. Confirm delivery area.
  3. Check normal price after the intro offer.
  4. Read skip and cancel rules.

## Why it matters

This improves trust and confidence, not just navigation. A visitor can understand the buying logic before clicking any offer.
