# Every Meal Guide Affiliate And Partnership Operating Pack

This pack exists to make Every Meal Guide cash-ready without pretending affiliate approvals already exist.

## Rule of the road

No application, email, DM, form submission, or tracking-link replacement happens without Darren explicitly approving that exact action.

## Commercial objective

Turn Every Meal Guide's comparison pages, country pages, review pages, and `/go/` routes into approved affiliate and direct partnership relationships.

The revenue path is:

1. Public comparison pages earn buyer-intent traffic.
2. Visitors click tracked `/go/` routes.
3. Approved affiliate URLs replace placeholder partner pages.
4. Direct partnerships fill gaps where affiliate networks do not exist.
5. Weekly offer checks keep high-intent pages fresh and trustworthy.

## Application readiness checklist

Before applying to any program, confirm:

- Public domain is live: `https://www.everymealguide.com/`.
- Contact email works and can receive program emails.
- Affiliate disclosure page is live.
- Privacy page is live.
- Methodology page is live.
- At least ten polished money pages are live.
- Brand review or comparison page exists for the target brand.
- `/go/{brand}/` route exists and does not redirect to an unapproved merchant URL.
- Application copy does not claim traffic, revenue, approvals, testing, or first-hand reviews unless proven.

## Safe application positioning

Use this positioning:

Every Meal Guide is a consumer meal-delivery comparison site helping shoppers choose between meal kits, prepared meals, high-protein meal prep, budget dinner boxes, diet-specific meals, family meals, frozen meals, and grocery dinner shortcuts by country, routine, cost, and current offer.

## Application copy

Short description:

Every Meal Guide helps consumers compare meal delivery services by meal type, country, budget, diet, household, prep time, and current offer. We cover meal kits, prepared meals, high-protein meal prep, family meals, budget boxes, vegan/keto/gluten-free options, frozen meals, and grocery dinner shortcuts.

Promotional method:

SEO comparison pages, review pages, country guides, deal pages, brand-vs-brand pages, email list capture, and clearly marked affiliate links.

Traffic statement:

New site. We are building a source-led comparison engine and will scale through buyer-intent SEO pages such as best meal delivery services, prepared meal delivery, meal kits, high-protein meal delivery, country guides, and brand-vs-brand comparisons.

Compliance statement:

Affiliate links are marked as sponsored where appropriate. We do not use fake ratings or invented offer claims. Availability, pricing, discounts, and subscription terms are checked against public sources and final checkout pages where possible.

## Direct partnership fallback

Use direct outreach when:

- No affiliate network program is visible.
- The brand is regional but relevant to country pages.
- The brand is a high-protein, prepared-meal, kids-meal, frozen-meal, or diet specialist with strong buyer intent.
- The brand has local demand but no obvious public partner page.

## Status definitions

- `not_started`: no application or outreach drafted yet.
- `ready_to_apply`: site and page evidence exist; waiting for Darren approval to submit.
- `applied`: application submitted by human or explicitly approved browser action.
- `approved`: program accepted Every Meal Guide.
- `rejected`: program declined or route unavailable.
- `direct_outreach_ready`: no obvious affiliate program; direct email can be sent after approval.
- `tracking_url_added`: approved tracking URL has been added to the site.
- `live_cash_route`: `/go/` page redirects through approved tracking route and records click events.

## Weekly monetization loop

1. Pick ten brands from the affiliate tracker.
2. Confirm whether each has an affiliate program or partner contact.
3. Move eligible brands to `ready_to_apply`.
4. Ask Darren for approval before submitting.
5. When approved, add tracking URLs and regenerate pages.
6. Log the first click and first conversion separately.
7. Revisit rejected brands with direct outreach only when they have meaningful traffic potential.

## Evidence boundary

This pack is preparation. It is not proof that any application was submitted, any program approved Every Meal Guide, any tracking URL is active, or any revenue has been earned.
