# Every Meal Guide Post-Launch Indexing And Measurement Checklist

Use this checklist after Darren approves deployment of the latest local build.

## Phase 1 - Deploy current local build

- Deploy the current local project to Vercel.
- Confirm `https://www.everymealguide.com/` returns the latest homepage.
- Confirm `https://www.everymealguide.com/sitemap.xml` loads.
- Confirm `https://www.everymealguide.com/robots.txt` loads.
- Confirm `https://www.everymealguide.com/googlea432a8df6c28372f.html` works for Google verification.

## Phase 2 - Google Search Console

- Verified URL-prefix property: `https://www.everymealguide.com/`.
- Submitted `https://www.everymealguide.com/sitemap.xml`.
- Confirmed sitemap processed successfully with 51 discovered pages and 0 videos on 24 June 2026.
- Wait for Page indexing and Performance reports to finish processing.
- Inspect the homepage.
- Inspect `https://www.everymealguide.com/best/meal-delivery-services/`.
- Inspect `https://www.everymealguide.com/best/prepared-meal-delivery/`.
- Inspect `https://www.everymealguide.com/best/meal-kits/`.
- Inspect `https://www.everymealguide.com/deals/best-meal-delivery-deals/`.
- Request indexing for only the highest-priority commercial pages first.

## Phase 3 - Analytics

- Choose GA4, Plausible, Vercel Analytics, or a combined setup.
- Confirm page views are recorded.
- Confirm `matcher_submit` is recorded.
- Confirm `affiliate_click` is recorded.
- Confirm `newsletter_interest` is recorded.
- Confirm events include path and brand where relevant.

## Phase 4 - Affiliate applications

- Confirm contact email works.
- Confirm affiliate disclosure is live.
- Confirm methodology page is live.
- Confirm privacy page is live.
- Submit first batch applications only after Darren approves.
- Log submission date, network, program, and expected review window.

## Phase 5 - First cash-route activation

- Wait for program approval.
- Add approved tracking URL.
- Keep `rel="sponsored nofollow"`.
- Deploy the updated route.
- Click-test only enough to confirm the redirect works.
- Record first site-side click.
- Record first affiliate-dashboard click separately.
- Record first commission separately.
