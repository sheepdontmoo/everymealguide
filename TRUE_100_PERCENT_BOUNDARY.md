# Every Meal Guide True 100% Boundary

Status date: 2026-06-23

Purpose: prevent local preparation from being mistaken for a completed, live, monetized site.

## Current Position

Every Meal Guide is very close as a local build. The remaining work is not more scaffolding. The remaining work requires browser evidence, public deployment decisions, external account setup, and affiliate approvals.

## Local Package Can Be Considered Ready When

1. Static pages build locally.
2. Brand accountability is present for every company.
3. Fresh N Lean remains watchlist-only.
4. Money pages, comparison pages, review pages, offer checks, company accountability, and comparison hub exist.
5. Affiliate redirect scaffolding exists.
6. Analytics event scaffolding exists.
7. Production env precheck exists.
8. Public launch approval packet exists.
9. Visual QA report scaffold exists.
10. Weekly growth reporting scaffold exists.

## The Full Goal Is Not Complete Until

1. Browser-led visual QA is completed on desktop and mobile.
2. Visual issues found in QA are fixed or explicitly accepted.
3. Final domain is chosen and approved.
4. Contact email is real and approved.
5. Site is publicly deployed.
6. Domain and HTTPS work.
7. Homepage and top money pages are live.
8. `sitemap.xml` and `robots.txt` are live.
9. Search Console is verified and sitemap is submitted.
10. Analytics provider is approved and connected.
11. `page_view` events are visible in analytics.
12. `affiliate_click` events are visible in analytics.
13. P0 affiliate applications are submitted after the live site is credible.
14. At least one affiliate program is approved.
15. At least one real approved affiliate URL is added through `/go/{brand}/`.
16. `/go/{brand}/` redirect is confirmed live.
17. Merchant dashboard shows at least one visit, conversion, sale, or commission signal before revenue proof is claimed.

## Exact Next Approval Needed

To move beyond local preparation, the next useful approval is:

```text
Approve browser visual QA for Every Meal Guide local preview.
```

After visual QA passes, the next approval is:

```text
Approve public launch prep for Every Meal Guide on [domain] using [provider].
```

## Do Not Claim

Do not claim:

1. The site is live.
2. The site is monetized.
3. Affiliate approval exists.
4. Revenue proof exists.
5. Visual QA passed.
6. Search Console is connected.
7. Analytics is live.

until the matching evidence exists.
