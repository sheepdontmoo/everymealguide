## What this means

The URL volume needed to reach 10,000+ exists in the planning layer, but most candidates should not be published yet.

The next real constraint is not only page generation. It is verified brand depth per country/topic so pages can give a useful 60-second answer.

## Quality rule

Candidate URLs are not public pages.

A candidate should only become a public page after:

- enough verified brands exist for the country/topic
- the page has a clear 60-second answer
- the page links to review pages and `/go/` offer checks
- public copy passes internal-wording guards
- the page is useful for a consumer, not only an SEO footprint
