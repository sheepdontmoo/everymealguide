## Daily Operating Loop

Each day:

1. Check Search Console sitemap, Pages, and Performance.
2. Check analytics if installed.
3. Polish the next highest-priority queue page.
4. Add clear internal links to best, country, review, comparison, deal, and `/go/` routes.
5. Add concise AI-citation sections that answer buyer questions directly.
6. Record what changed in `daily/YYYY-MM-DD.md`.
7. Do not claim traffic, indexing, affiliate approval, or revenue without dashboard evidence.

## Evidence Boundaries

- `affiliate_click` before approval means buyer intent, not revenue.
- Sitemap discovery means Google found URLs, not that they are indexed.
- URL inspection/request indexing means submitted for consideration, not guaranteed ranking.
- AI-citation-ready content means structured for retrieval, not proven citations.
- Revenue starts only when an affiliate or payment dashboard confirms it.

## Go / No-Go Gates

Green:

- Sitemap stays processed.
- No robots/canonical/indexability blockers.
- Analytics records real page views and events.
- Priority pages are polished and internally linked.

Yellow:

- Search Console still processing after 48-72 hours.
- Some priority pages discovered but not indexed.
- Analytics installed but event mapping incomplete.

Red:

- Sitemap fetch fails again and detail page no longer shows success.
- Robots/canonical blocks priority pages.
- Public pages 404 or redirect unexpectedly.
- `/go/` routes imply affiliate approval before approvals exist.
