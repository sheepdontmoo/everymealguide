## Evidence boundary

Nothing has been submitted.

A row should not be marked submitted without proof such as:

- confirmation email
- affiliate-network application record
- submitted form screenshot
- sent email record
- network status page

A row should not be marked approved unless the brand, network, or direct partner explicitly approves Every Meal Guide.

No affiliate URL should be added to `APPROVED_AFFILIATE_LINKS.json` unless approval is confirmed.

## Why this matters

This gives Every Meal Guide a clean operating system for the 100+ partner application milestone without blurring drafts, submissions, approvals, and monetized routes.
