# Every Meal Guide First Money-Page Briefs

## Publishing Principle

Every money page should answer one buyer question quickly, then prove the answer with fit criteria, current deal caution, alternatives, and a clear outbound next step.

## P0 Brief: Best Meal Delivery Services

Target URL: `/best/meal-delivery-services/`

Primary intent: buyer wants the best service overall but may not know whether they need meal kits or prepared meals.

Required sections:

1. Quick answer: best overall, best prepared, best budget, best for families.
2. Match by routine: no-cook, cook-with-help, family, budget, high-protein, vegetarian.
3. Ranking cards with pros, watch-outs, delivery/country notes, and CTA.
4. Meal kits vs prepared meals table.
5. First-box deal warning: compare box two, shipping, skip rules.
6. Methodology link and affiliate disclosure.
7. FAQ with direct answers for snippets.

Evidence needed before stronger copy:

1. Current first-order offer language from each brand.
2. Current delivery-country availability.
3. Current price ranges or "from" prices, only if verified.
4. Affiliate program approval status.

## P0 Brief: Best Meal Delivery Deals

Target URL: `/deals/best-meal-delivery-deals/`

Primary intent: buyer is close to ordering and wants the biggest sensible discount.

Required sections:

1. Deal table: brand, meal type, best for, current offer, second-order caution, CTA.
2. "Biggest discount is not always best" explainer.
3. Cancellation/skip deadline checklist.
4. Country filter notes.
5. Brand-specific deal cards for HelloFresh, Factor, Gousto, CookUnity.

Implemented locally:

1. Reusable deal decision table with headline offer check, best buyer, second-box risk, checkout check, and CTA.
2. Source-backed latest brand checks.
3. Buyer warnings that avoid treating intro discounts as evergreen value.

Do not publish exact coupon numbers unless verified the same week.

## P0 Brief: Factor vs CookUnity

3. Who should avoid each.
4. Alternatives: Fresh N Lean, prepared meal hub, high-protein hub.

Evidence needed:

1. Current service-area notes.
2. Current menu/nutrition positioning from brand pages.
3. Current public pricing or plan language.
