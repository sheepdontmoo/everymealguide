# Every Meal Guide 30-Page Expansion Roadmap

## Purpose

Build a focused SEO moat around commercial meal-delivery comparison searches before expanding into lower-intent guides.

Metric status: provisional. Add live keyword volume, CPC, difficulty, and SERP notes later.

## Publishing Rule

Prioritize pages that can produce affiliate intent:

1. Brand vs brand.
2. Best service for a buyer situation.
3. Deal/coupon pages with subscription-risk warnings.
4. Reviews with alternatives.
5. Country pages where recommendations differ.

Avoid recipe content until the comparison/deal/review cluster has traction.

## Content Depth Requirements

Each P0/P1 money page should include:

1. Quick answer in first screen.
2. Who should choose each service.
3. Who should skip each service.
4. Deal warning or second-box risk.
5. Current-source check notes.
6. Internal links to the closest alternative.
7. Affiliate CTA or holding-page CTA.
8. FAQ with structured data.

## Internal Link Rules

1. Every brand review links to:
   - best overall hub
   - best prepared/meal-kit hub
   - relevant vs page
   - relevant deal page
2. Every vs page links to:
   - both brand reviews
   - relevant best page
   - best deals page
3. Every best page links to:
   - deals page
   - top brand reviews
   - related best pages
4. Every deal page links to:
   - best overall hub
   - relevant brand reviews
   - methodology
   - affiliate disclosure
