## Editorial caveats

- Complete nutrition and meal replacement brands must not be presented as cooked dinner delivery.
- Baby/toddler food brands should sit under parent and kids food pages, not adult meal-prep rankings.
- Weight-loss and diet replacement pages need clear health disclaimers and should avoid medical outcome claims.
- Inactive brands such as Ambronite and uncertain brands such as Runtime/Yumble should be used for alternatives intent only until active checkout is proven.
