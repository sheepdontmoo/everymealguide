## Editorial caveats

- Protein boxes, produce boxes, hummus/snack bundles, and smoothie packets must be treated as dinner helpers or adjacent grocery routes, not full prepared meal delivery.
- Private chef marketplaces should sit in alternatives pages and not be ranked beside ordinary meal kits unless the page explicitly compares service types.
- Regional routes need delivery-area caveats until postcode/browser checks are run.
