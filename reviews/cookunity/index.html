CookUnity FAQ

Is CookUnity a meal kit or a prepared meal service?

CookUnity is a prepared meal service. Chef-made meals arrive fully cooked — no prep or cooking required, just reheat and eat. This is different from meal kit services like HelloFresh or EveryPlate where you cook from scratch.

Is CookUnity available outside the United States?

CookUnity primarily operates in the United States. It is not currently available in the UK, Ireland, Canada, or Australia. Check the CookUnity website for the latest delivery coverage map, as this can change.

How does CookUnity differ from Factor?

Both are US prepared meal services, but they have different strengths. CookUnity focuses on chef variety — meals are made by a rotating roster of professional chefs, with wider cuisine diversity. Factor focuses on high-protein, macro-friendly meals. CookUnity meals are typically more expensive per serving.

What does CookUnity cost after the introductory offer?

CookUnity's introductory offer (typically 50% off the first week) is significantly cheaper than ongoing pricing. After the first week, per-meal cost depends on how many meals per week you select. Check the current recurring price at checkout before committing.

What should I compare against CookUnity?

Compare CookUnity against Factor for a lower-cost US prepared meal alternative, and against Home Chef for a mixed meal kit and prepared meal option. See our best prepared meal delivery guide for a full comparison.
