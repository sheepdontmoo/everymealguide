Gousto FAQ

Is Gousto available outside the UK?

No. Gousto is a UK-only meal kit service. It does not deliver to Ireland, the US, Canada, or Australia. If you're outside the UK, see your country's guide: Ireland, United States, Australia.

Is Gousto available in Ireland?

No. Gousto operates exclusively in the United Kingdom and does not currently deliver to the Republic of Ireland. For Irish meal kit options, check our Ireland meal delivery guide.

Does Gousto deliver in Northern Ireland?

Gousto's coverage in Northern Ireland is limited. Check your specific postcode directly on the Gousto website before ordering — rural and remote postcodes may not be covered.

Is Gousto a meal kit or a prepared meal service?

Gousto is a meal kit service. You receive pre-portioned fresh ingredients and a recipe card, then cook the meal yourself. Most Gousto recipes take 10–40 minutes from start to finish.

What does Gousto cost after the first box discount?

Gousto typically offers 65% or more off the first box. After that, the per-recipe price depends on the number of recipes per week and your plan type. Always compare the second-box price before committing to the subscription.

What should I compare against Gousto?

Compare Gousto against HelloFresh if you want a global meal kit brand, and against Mindful Chef or Green Chef if diet-specific options matter. All three are available in the UK.
