HelloFresh FAQ

Is HelloFresh a meal kit or a prepared meal service?

HelloFresh is a meal kit service — you receive pre-portioned fresh ingredients and a recipe card, then cook the meal yourself. Typical prep time is 20–40 minutes. If you want no-cook dinners, look at prepared meal services like Factor or CookUnity instead.

Is HelloFresh available in Ireland?

Yes, HelloFresh delivers in Ireland. Check your exact postcode on the HelloFresh website — rural coverage can vary. For more Irish meal delivery options, see our Ireland meal delivery guide.

Does HelloFresh deliver in Australia?

Yes. HelloFresh operates in Australia with localised menus and pricing. Plans and prices differ from the US and UK versions. See our Australia meal delivery guide for more local options.

Is HelloFresh available in Canada?

Yes, HelloFresh delivers in Canada. Check the Canadian HelloFresh site for current pricing in CAD and available plans for your province.

What does HelloFresh cost after the first discount?

HelloFresh's introductory offer typically covers your first 2–3 boxes at a steep discount. After that, the per-meal price depends on your plan size and meals per week. Always check the recurring price, not just the welcome offer, before committing.

Can I skip or cancel HelloFresh?

Yes, but you need to skip or cancel before the weekly cut-off deadline — typically 5 days before your next delivery date. Log into your account and check your upcoming delivery schedule and skip deadline.

What should I compare against HelloFresh?

Compare HelloFresh against EveryPlate if price per meal matters most, against Gousto (UK only) for more recipe variety, and against Factor or CookUnity if you'd prefer meals that arrive already cooked.
