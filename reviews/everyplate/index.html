EveryPlate FAQ

Is EveryPlate a meal kit or a prepared meal service?

EveryPlate is a meal kit service. You receive fresh pre-portioned ingredients and a recipe card, then cook the meal yourself. It is not a prepared meal service — if you want meals that arrive already cooked, look at Factor or CookUnity instead.

Is EveryPlate available in the UK?

No. EveryPlate is a US-only service. It does not deliver to the UK, Ireland, Canada, or Australia. For UK budget meal kit alternatives, see our UK meal delivery guide.

Is EveryPlate available in Australia?

No. EveryPlate only delivers in the United States. For budget meal kit options in Australia, check our Australia meal delivery guide.

What is the difference between EveryPlate and HelloFresh?

EveryPlate and HelloFresh are both part of the HelloFresh Group, but serve different budgets. EveryPlate is the budget option: fewer weekly recipes, simpler packaging, and a lower per-serving price. HelloFresh offers more menu variety, premium ingredients, and more flexibility in plan customisation.

What does EveryPlate cost after the introductory offer?

EveryPlate is consistently one of the lowest-cost US meal kit services, even after the intro discount ends. The recurring per-meal price depends on your plan size, but it is typically lower than HelloFresh and most competitors. Check the second-box price at checkout before committing.

What should I compare against EveryPlate?

Compare EveryPlate against HelloFresh if you want more recipe variety at a slightly higher price, and against Dinnerly for another budget-focused meal kit. If you mainly want to avoid cooking altogether, look at prepared meal services like Factor.
