Choose Factor if You want zero cooking on weeknights

Factor is for people who want fully cooked meals that take 2 minutes to reheat. Especially strong if you track protein or macros — most plans have macro labels per meal.

Skip it if You're outside the US or want to cook

Factor only delivers in the United States. If you're in the UK, Ireland, Australia, or Canada — it's not an option. If you enjoy cooking, a meal kit is a better fit.

Compare against CookUnity, Home Chef, HelloFresh

CookUnity if you want more chef variety at a similar price. Home Chef for a mix of prep and heat-and-eat. HelloFresh if you want to cook and spend less per meal.

What it is Prepared meals — you just reheat them

Factor delivers fully cooked, chef-designed meals that take 2 minutes in the microwave or a few minutes in the oven. No cooking, no chopping, no cleanup beyond the container.

Where it delivers United States only

Factor is a US-only service. It does not deliver to the UK, Ireland, Canada, or Australia. If you're outside the US, see our country guides for local prepared meal options.

The real catch $10.99 delivery every week on all plans

Factor charges a flat $10.99/week delivery fee regardless of plan size. On the 4-meal plan, that adds ~$2.75 per meal on top of the meal price. Ordering 8+ meals/week spreads the fee further.

Skip and cancel

How to skip or cancel Factor

To skip a week Skip by Wednesday 11:59pm CT — for Friday delivery

Factor delivers on Friday. You must skip by 11:59pm Central Time the Wednesday before. Log into your Factor account → Upcoming Deliveries → Skip Week. Missing this deadline means the charge goes through.

To cancel Cancel via account settings or live chat — no contract

Log in → Account → Plan → Cancel Plan. If you can't find the option, use the Factor live chat. Cancel before the Wednesday cut-off for your next scheduled delivery. No cancellation fee.

Watch out Factor may offer a retention discount when you try to cancel

Factor often presents a discount offer (e.g. 30% off next box) when you initiate a cancellation. Decide in advance whether you want to pause vs cancel permanently — declining the offer completes cancellation.

Factor FAQ

Is Factor a meal kit or a prepared meal service?

Factor is a prepared meal service — meals arrive fully cooked and only need reheating. There is no prep, chopping, or cooking involved. This is different from meal kit services like HelloFresh, where you cook from raw ingredients.

Is Factor available in the United Kingdom?

No. Factor is a US-based service and does not currently deliver to the UK. If you're in the UK and want no-cook prepared meals, check our UK meal delivery guide for locally available options.

Does Factor deliver in Canada or Australia?

Factor currently operates only in the United States. For prepared meal delivery in Canada or Australia, see our Canada and Australia country guides.

What does Factor cost after the first discount?

Factor's introductory offer is heavily discounted — typically 50% off the first box. After the first box, expect ~$11–15/meal before delivery (the 4-meal plan is ~$15/meal; the 18-meal plan is ~$11/meal), plus a flat $10.99/week delivery fee on all plans. The delivery fee is the same regardless of how many meals you order.

Can I skip or cancel Factor easily?

Factor allows skipping and cancelling, but there are weekly cut-off deadlines. If you miss the skip deadline, you'll be charged for that week's box. Check your account's cancellation and skip rules before the weekly deadline.

What should I compare against Factor?

Compare Factor against CookUnity and Home Chef if you want prepared meals in the US. For a broader comparison, see our best prepared meal delivery page.
