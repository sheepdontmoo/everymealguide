60-second rule

How to choose without getting trapped by a shiny first-box deal.

Most bad meal-delivery choices happen because people compare discounts before they compare routine fit. Use this order before opening any offer.

  1. 01 Pick the format

    Prepared meals if you want no cooking. Meal kits if you still want fresh cooking.

  2. 02 Confirm delivery area

    A great national brand is useless if the menu, postcode, or ZIP code does not work for you.

  3. 03 Check the normal price

    The first box can be cheap. The real decision is what week two, three, and four cost.

  4. 04 Read skip and cancel rules

    Choose the service you can pause easily when travel, budget, or appetite changes.
